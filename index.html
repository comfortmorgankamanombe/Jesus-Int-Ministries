About Our Ministry

Spreading the Gospel to all nations

The founder of JESUS International Ministries is Jesus Christ. He is the one that led the foundation of the church. Prophet J. Chikambure is the general overseer of JESUS International Ministries from 2020.

Our mission is to preach the Gospel, heal the brokenhearted, and set the captives free through the power of Jesus Christ. We operate under the divine mandate to take the message of salvation to all nations.

We welcome visitors from every part of Zimbabwe and all over the world. We offer airport pickups and visa assistance for international visitors.
