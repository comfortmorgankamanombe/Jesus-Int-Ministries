About Our Ministry

Spreading the Gospel to all nations

JESUS International Ministries was founded in 2020 by Prophet J. Chikambure under the spiritual covering of T.B. Joshua. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives and communities through the message of Christ.

Our mission is to preach the Gospel, heal the brokenhearted, and set the captives free through the power of Jesus Christ. We operate under the divine mandate to take the message of salvation to all nations.
